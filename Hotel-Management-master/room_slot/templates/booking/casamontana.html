Casa Montana

Casa Montana is the perfect destination for those who want to get away from the bustle of life, for those who want a marvelous interlude of pleasure but also for those looking for a more relaxed and peaceful lifestyle. A blend of country cosines and smart elegance: the Hotel Casa Montana is built in modern style, in a quiet yet central location of Munnar. Whatever you’re idea of a holiday, full of activities or just doing nothing, staying in this hotel will fulfil your every desire. Footpaths and cross-country trails are just round the corner, and it’s only a stone’s throw to the town centre
