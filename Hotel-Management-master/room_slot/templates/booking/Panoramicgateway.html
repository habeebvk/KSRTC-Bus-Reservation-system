The Panoramic Gateway

Offering two rooftop heated swimming pools separate for adults and families, two restaurants and a spa and wellness center, The Panoramic Getaway is located in scenic hill station of Munnar. The Blossom Park is 10 km and the Mattupetty Dam and the Eravikulam National Park are 20 km. The Munnar Bus Station is 10 km, the Ernakulam South Railway Station and the Cochin International Airport are 120 km.Each air-conditioned room here will provide you with a cable TV, a seating area and a balcony. Featuring a shower, private bathroom also comes with free toiletries and slippers. You can enjoy mountain view from the room.
