Malappuram-Ooty
Per head Rs1000/- 3 days
Ooty is a popular hill station located in the Nilgiri Hills. Ootacamund was rated the best hill station
in means of hospitality and nature passing.Ooty officially known as Udhagamandalam (also known as Ootacamund) , is a city and a
municipality in the Nilgiris district of the South Indian state of Tamil Nadu. It is located 86 km (53 mi) north west
of Coimbatore and 128 km (80 mi) south of Mysore and is the headquarters of the Nilgiris district.
It is a popular hill station located in the Nilgiri Hills. It is popularly called the Queen of Hill Stations.
It was the summer capital of the Madras Presidency.Ooty is situated in the Nilgiri Biosphere Reserve. Many of the forested areas
and water bodies are off-limits to most visitors in order to protect this fragile ecosystem. Some areas of the Biosphere Reserve have
been earmarked for tourism development, and steps are being undertaken to open these areas to visitors whilst conserving the area.
It is situated at an altitude of 2,240 metres (7,350 feet) above sea level.
